Privacy Policy

SCH CAD Drafting Services
Sydney, NSW, Australia

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our drafting services or interact with our website. We follow the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that helps us provide drafting services. This may include:

  • Personal information such as your name, company name, email address, and phone number
  • Project files and documents you provide, including PDFs, drawings, plans, sketches, and reference materials
  • Website usage information such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and browser type

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Communicate with you
  • Prepare and deliver drafting work
  • Review and update project details
  • Improve our website and services
  • Keep internal records

We do not sell or trade your information.

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure. This includes secure digital storage, limited access to project files, industry-standard security measures, and regular system updates. No online system is completely risk-free, but we work to reduce risks wherever possible.

5. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information only when necessary, such as with trusted service providers or when required by Australian law or a court order. We do not share your information overseas unless needed for service delivery, and we will let you know if this happens.

6. Access and Corrections

You can request access to the personal information we hold about you and ask us to correct any inaccurate details. Contact us using the details below.

7. Cookies and Website Tracking

Our website may use cookies to improve your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

8. Uploaded Documents

Any files you upload or send to us are used only for drafting purposes. We do not reuse or publish your project materials without your permission.

9. Third-Party Websites

Our website may link to other sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
